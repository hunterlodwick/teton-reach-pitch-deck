Common Questions

Great questions to think about. Here's what sets us apart from the rest.

Is the quality really good at $10 per hour?

Yes. Callers are sourced from the top 1% of applicants and undergo rigorous training. The rate reflects geographic wage efficiency — not quality compromise.

How are leads qualified?

Every lead goes through a structured qualification process. Motivation, timeline, and fit are verified before it ever reaches your calendar.

Will you integrate with my CRM?

All leads are delivered via Google Sheets for full transparency, plus weekly check-ins to make sure the campaign is running exactly how you want it.

Is this compliant with TCPA and DNC?

Absolutely. All data is run through DNC scrubbing and adheres to TCPA regulations at both federal and state levels.

How fast can we start?

Typical onboarding is 3–5 business days. If we get paperwork signed on Friday, we can more than likely get you started by Monday.

Do I need to bring my own data?

No. Custom targeted data lists are built for you — up to 12,000 records tailored to your market. Already have data? That works too.

When do billable hours start?

Your billable hours don't start until your first lead comes in. You'll never pay for setup time or ramp-up — only for active, productive calling.
